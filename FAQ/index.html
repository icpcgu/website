General

The ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Contest) community refers to a global network of students, coaches, faculty advisors, and professionals who participate in or support the ICPC programming competitions and related activities. The ICPC is one of the most prestigious and well-known competitive programming events in the world. Here's an overview of the ICPC community.

Follow This Link.

No, you do not need prior programming experience to join the ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Contest), but it certainly helps. The ICPC is open to undergraduate and postgraduate students from universities and colleges around the world, and participants range from beginners to advanced programmers.

It is free for all Galala students.

The primary format of the ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Contest) is a team competition, where teams of three students from the same university.

Call Anyone on the team.

We will make a session for each topic and sessions for practice and solve some problems for each topic.
